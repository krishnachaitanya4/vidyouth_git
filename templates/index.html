About The Event

VIDYOUTH-2k23-UCEN. This is the 4th national-level technical symposium. This year we are planning to organize many interesting and fruitful technical and non-technical events in which participants will be from different engineering colleges present in the districts palnadu, Guntur, Prakasam,bapatla, and Krishna. We the students of the EEE department have IEEE membership where we have access to national and international conferences and also communicate with students from different regions. In this technical symposium there will be 3000 to 4000 people gathering in our college. Students can learn a lot many things and develop their skills by participating in this technical symposium.
